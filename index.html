Prototype System

Core Concept

A pre-War military research project attempted to preserve critical manufacturing capability after a catastrophic event.

Rather than storing finished weapons and equipment, the project focused on preserving the ability to rebuild lost technology.

The player does not find a new weapon.

The player restores the ability to create one.

The Reconstruction Fabricator is a damaged prototype manufacturing system. It requires recovered components, recovered research data, and recovered schematics before it can produce anything meaningful.

The system was abandoned before completion.
The player finishes the work.

Entry 001: Project Overview

The purpose of Project P.H.A.N.T.O.M. was to preserve critical defense manufacturing capability in the event of catastrophic infrastructure failure.

Current military doctrine assumes equipment loss can be compensated through replacement production.

This assumption is invalid.
  • Industrial capacity is vulnerable.
  • Supply chains are vulnerable.
  • Manufacturing expertise is vulnerable.

Project P.H.A.N.T.O.M. preserves the ability to restore lost equipment from recovered patterns and raw materials.

Recipe System

The Reconstruction Fabricator does not use traditional recipes. It restores archived manufacturing patterns.

Pattern terminology matters.
The machine does not cook food or assemble weapons. It reconstructs lost production methods.
